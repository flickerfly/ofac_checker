ofac_checker by codemis

This is designed to assist in maintaining compliance with the Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Asset Control. They maintain a list called the Specially Designated Nationals list. OFAC requires that institutions moving money internationally scan the transactions prior to transferring the funds for any matches against this list.
A Ruby script that looks through your .ach files, and compares the payees with the current OFAC list. It will generate a CSV file with the results, and notify you when it has been completed by email.
Disclaimer: This code is not provided with any type of a warranty and may not solve your problem of compliance. It is provided as-is and should be evaluated thoroughly for meeting your own compliance needs.
